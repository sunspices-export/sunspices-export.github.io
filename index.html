At Sun Spices India, we pride ourselves on being a premier merchant exporter of the finest Indian spices. Our roots are deeply embedded in the spice-rich terrains of Kerala, ensuring that we bring only the most authentic produce to the global market.

Our commitment to excellence drives us to source cardamom and whole spices directly from dedicated farmers in Vandanmedu and surrounding areas. This direct link ensures that every batch maintains its natural essential oils and superior aroma profile.

We understand the stringent requirements of international B2B buyers. Therefore, our processing and packaging standards are designed to preserve the freshness and longevity of our spices, meeting global safety and quality certifications.

Integrity, transparency, and consistency are the pillars of Sun Spices India. Whether you are looking for premium green cardamom or a variety of whole spices, we provide reliable supply chain solutions tailored to your business needs.
