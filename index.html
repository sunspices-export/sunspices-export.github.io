The Queen of Spices

Our Alleppey Green Extra Bold (AGEB) cardamom pods represent the pinnacle of Kerala's harvest. Sourced from the mist-covered plantations of Vandanmedu, these pods are prized globally for their vibrant green color and intense essential oil content. We strictly adhere to size-grading ranging from 7mm to 8mm+ to ensure only the finest reaches our clients.

The King of Spice: Malabar Pepper

Directly sourced from the heart of Idukki, our black pepper (TGEB) is sun-dried to perfection, retaining its natural piperine levels. Known for its bold aroma and sharp heat, it meets the highest standards for European and Middle Eastern markets. Every batch undergoes rigorous quality checks for density and purity before export.
