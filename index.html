The Queen of Spices: Alleppey Green

Our Cardamom represents the soul of Kerala's high-altitude plantations. Sourced from the misty valleys of Vandanmedu, these pods are harvested at peak maturity to lock in the intense essential oil content (cineole). We strictly adhere to size-grading (7mm to 8mm+) ensuring a vibrant green hue that meets the most demanding standards of Gulf and European markets.

The unique micro-climate of the Western Ghats provides the perfect moisture and soil acidity, resulting in pods that are larger, greener, and more aromatic than any other variety in the world.

Black Gold: The King of Malabar

Sourced from the heart of Idukki, our TGEB (Tellicherry Garbled Extra Bold) black pepper is sun-dried to a deep, obsidian black. We ensure zero moisture retention and a high piperine level, providing that signature sharp heat and complex woody aroma. Every grain is Sortex-cleaned to remove all stalks and stones.

Grown under the natural canopy of tropical trees, our pepper vines benefit from organic humus-rich soil, ensuring a product that is as healthy as it is flavorful.

Uncompromising Quality Protocol

At Sun Spices India, quality is our heritage. From farm-gate procurement to final vacuum-sealed packaging, every batch undergoes multi-level screening. We provide full traceability with Phytosanitary, Health, and Lab certifications tailored to your country's specific food safety regulations.

We believe in "New Crop" freshness. Our strategically located warehouse in Vandanmedu allows us to process and ship within days of the harvest, bypassing long storage cycles that degrade aroma.

The Vandanmedu Heritage

Vandanmedu is the world's largest auction center for cardamom. Being rooted here gives us a strategic edge in market intelligence and quality selection. We bridge the gap between traditional Kerala farming and modern B2B global trade with professional precision and complete transparency.

Our long-standing relationships with plantation owners ensure we get the "First Pick" of every harvest, allowing us to offer prices that are competitive without ever sacrificing purity.
