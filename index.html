The Vandanmedu Terroir

Sun Spices India is born in the mist-shrouded elevations of Vandanmedu, Idukki—the cardamom capital of the world. The unique combination of acidic soil, 2500mm of annual rainfall, and the shade of ancient evergreen trees creates a micro-climate where our cardamom develops its unmatched essential oil density. We don't just export; we share the soul of the Western Ghats.
