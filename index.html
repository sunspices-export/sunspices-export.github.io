The Queen of Vandanmedu

Strategically headquartered in Vandanmedu, the global epicenter of cardamom trade, Sun Spices India leverages the unique high-altitude mist and fertile volcanic soil of the Idukki district. The King’s Domain: Malabar Gold

Our Black Pepper (TGEB) is sourced from ancestral plantations where the climate remains consistently humid, perfect for piperine development. We avoid industrial mechanical drying, opting for the traditional sun-drying method on natural mats. This preservation of heritage ensures a sharp, bold heat that meets the strict EU and Middle Eastern quality benchmarks.
