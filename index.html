The Vandanmedu Legacy

Our Alleppey Green Extra Bold (AGEB) cardamom is grown in the high-altitude, mist-drenched plantations of Vandanmedu, the spice capital of the world. The unique combination of acidic soil and tropical rainfall creates pods with the world's highest essential oil content. We hand-pick each pod at peak maturity to ensure that vibrant green color that global connoisseurs demand.

The King of Spices: Black Gold

Sun Spices India provides authentic Malabar Black Pepper (TGEB), sun-dried using traditional Kerala methods to lock in the intense piperine heat. Our processing units in Idukki ensure zero contamination, maintaining the density and pungency that define premium grade export pepper. Every shipment is a testament to the rich agricultural heritage of the Western Ghats.

Uncompromising Standards

Quality at Sun Spices India is monitored from the farm gate to the export container. Our state-of-the-art Sortex cleaning and vacuum packaging ensure that the "New Crop" freshness is preserved during long-distance sea freight. We comply with all international food safety regulations including FSSAI, Spices Board, and FDA standards for various global markets.

We believe in transparent global trade. By bridging the gap between traditional Kerala farmers and international B2B buyers, we ensure fair pricing for growers and premium quality for importers.
